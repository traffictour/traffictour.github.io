Traffic Tour

Traffic Tour is a free-to-play mobile racing game developed by Wolves Interactive. The game blends racing and traffic simulation, providing players with a realistic racing experience on urban roads.

Welcome to Traffic Tour, where the virtual world road awaits you! If you're a fan,simulations, and thrilling challenges, you've come to the right place. Get ready to embark on a journey into the world of virtual excitement!

What We Offer

At Traffic Tour, we provide an extensive collection of captivating games that cater to all types. Whether you're a seasoned gamer seeking the ultimate experience or a casual player looking for some fast-paced fun, Traffic Tour offers a wide range of games to satisfy your cravings for high-octane adventures.

Diverse Game Selection

Traffic Tour boasts an impressive selection of games, each designed to deliver unique and exhilarating gameplay experiences.our diverse range of games ensures there's something for everyone. Here are some of the exciting categories you can explore on our website:

Car Games

Whether you have a penchant for powerful sports cars, luxurious cars, or classic cars, Traffic Tour offers an array of options to suit your racing preferences. Choose from various game modes, including single-player championships, intense multiplayer races, and time trials to challenge yourself and improve your racing abilities. 

Racing Games

Experience the exhilaration of high-speed racing in our collection of intense racing games. We offer a variety of options to satisfy your racing desires including: Solo challenges against AI opponents or the thrill of multiplayer races. Take on the competition on diverse tracks and circuits, each with its own unique challenges and obstacles. Upgrade your vehicles, unlock new tracks, and customize your ride to create the ultimate racing machine. Challenge yourself in different game modes, from intense championships to thrilling time trials, as you strive for victory and aim to reach the top of the leaderboards. 

Drifting Games

With a wide range of drifting games that we offer to choose from, including realistic simulations and arcade-style experiences, you'll have plenty of opportunities to hone your drifting techniques and prove yourself as the ultimate drift king. Get ready to burn rubber as you showcase your style and earn points by executing jaw-dropping drifts around tight corners and challenging courses. Show off your ability to maintain perfect control while sliding sideways, leaving behind a trail of smoke and tire marks.

Parking Games

You'll face a variety of parking scenarios that will put your skills to the test. From tight spaces and crowded parking lots to parallel parking challenges. Each level presents a new and exciting parking challenge, pushing you to make perfect turns, control your speed, and showcase your expert handling.

Monster Truck Games

Who doesn't love the sheer excitement of monster trucks with their massive tires, screeching tires, and powerful engines? Our collection of thrilling monster truck games allows you to immerse yourself in the world of these incredible vehicles. Experience the thrill of crushing cars, performing jaw-dropping stunts, and engaging in exhilarating races against other monster trucks. At Traffic Tour, we offer a wide variety of truck and monster truck games that guarantee hours of non-stop fun and entertainment. From big trucks to pickup trucks, winter trucks to zombie trucks, extreme trucks to off-road trucks, the options are endless.
