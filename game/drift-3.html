Drift 3

Welcome to Drift 3, the ultimate drifting experience! Get ready to burn rubber and showcase your drifting skills in adrenaline-fueled races against skilled opponents.

What We Offer

Drift 3 offers a thrilling mix of high-speed racing and precision drifting. Take control of powerful drift cars and navigate through challenging tracks filled with hairpin turns, tight corners, and obstacles.

Gameplay

In Drift 3, your objective is to score points by executing stylish drifts around corners and maintaining control of your vehicle. The longer and more controlled your drifts, the higher your score.

Controls:

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your car.
  • Press the spacebar or Ctrl key to engage the handbrake and initiate drifts.
  • Balance throttle and steering to control the angle and speed of your drifts.

Features

  • Multiple drift cars to choose from, each with unique handling characteristics
  • Realistic physics simulation for authentic drifting experience
  • Varied tracks set in diverse locations, including city streets, mountain passes, and drift circuits
  • Upgrade and customize your cars with performance parts and visual modifications
  • Compete in drift challenges, time trials, and multiplayer drift battles

Get ready to master the art of drifting and become the ultimate drift king in Drift 3!
