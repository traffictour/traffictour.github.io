Fly Car Stun 3

Welcome to Fly Car Stun 3, where you can experience the thrill of flying and performing breathtaking stunts with futuristic cars! Prepare yourself for an adrenaline-fueled adventure as you navigate through challenging aerial courses.

What We Offer

Fly Car Stun 3 brings you the ultimate flying car stunt experience with advanced graphics, realistic physics, and exhilarating gameplay. Choose from a variety of futuristic flying cars and take to the skies in this high-flying racing extravaganza!

Gameplay

In Fly Car Stun 3, your objective is to pilot your flying car through a series of rings, obstacles, and loops while performing impressive stunts to earn points. Master the controls of your vehicle and push the limits of aerial acrobatics as you soar through the sky.

Controls:

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to control the direction of your flying car.
  • Press the spacebar or Ctrl key to activate the car's boosters and gain altitude.
  • Perform flips and rolls using the Q and E keys.

Features

  • Multiple challenging levels set in stunning environments
  • Upgrade and customize your flying car with advanced technology and aerodynamic enhancements
  • Compete against the clock or challenge friends in multiplayer stunt races
  • Unlock achievements and earn rewards for completing daring stunts and mastering each level
  • Dynamic weather and day/night cycle for added realism and immersion

Get ready to defy gravity and experience the excitement of aerial stunts in Fly Car Stun 3!
