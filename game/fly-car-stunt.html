Fly Car Stunt

Welcome to Fly Car Stunt, an exhilarating game where you take control of futuristic flying cars and perform daring stunts in the sky! Prepare yourself for an adrenaline-fueled experience as you defy gravity and push the limits of aerial acrobatics.

What We Offer

Fly Car Stunt offers a thrilling mix of high-flying action and precision stunts. Choose from a variety of cutting-edge flying cars and test your skills in challenging aerial courses set against stunning landscapes.

Gameplay

In Fly Car Stunt, your goal is to navigate your flying car through a series of rings, loops, and obstacles while performing daring stunts to earn points. Master the controls of your vehicle and showcase your aerial prowess as you soar through the skies.

Controls:

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to control the direction of your flying car.
  • Press the spacebar or Ctrl key to activate the car's boosters and gain altitude.
  • Perform flips and rolls using the Q and E keys.

Features

  • Multiple challenging levels set in breathtaking environments
  • Upgrade and customize your flying car with advanced technology and aerodynamic enhancements
  • Compete against the clock or challenge friends in multiplayer stunt races
  • Unlock achievements and earn rewards for completing daring stunts and mastering each level
  • Dynamic weather and day/night cycle for added realism and immersion

Get ready to take to the skies and experience the thrill of aerial stunts in Fly Car Stunt!
